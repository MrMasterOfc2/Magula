Booking Guidelines

  • TB Hall: Available for weddings, meetings, public events. Security deposit required.
  • Public Ground: For sports events, community gatherings, exhibitions.
  • JCB / Motorcator: For construction, road works, land development.
  • Water Bowser: For water supply during drought, construction, cleaning.
  • Submit request at least 7 days before the event date.
  • Approval subject to availability and council policies.
  • Fees and deposits must be paid upon approval.
After submission: You will receive a confirmation via WhatsApp when approved.
